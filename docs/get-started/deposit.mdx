# Deposit

You're out of EXEs, what do you do now? Easy, just head to the _Assets_ page (from the left sidebar) and deposit with your favorite cryptocurrency.

You can choose the cryptocurrency you want to top up with from Bitcoin, Ethereum, Dogecoin, USDT, or UTK. For USDT, you can either go for ERC20 or TRC20, which significantly reduces any transaction fees.

<Image src="/img/platform/deposit.png" alt="Deposit modal" />

After the transaction is confirmed your EXEs will be added to your balance.
